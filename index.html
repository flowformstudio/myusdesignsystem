01 — Foundation

Four principles behind every decision.

MyUS moves packages across borders. Our design moves people through that process — with clarity, speed, and the quiet confidence of a brand that's been doing this for decades.

01
Trust is the product.
Dark navy authority, warm orange moments. We earn trust by showing the work — weights, dimensions, tracking, costs.
02
Global by default.
Type that reads in 20 languages. Patterns that respect RTL. Currency, weights, and dates adapt silently to the member.
03
Density with air.
Package logistics are data-dense. We keep things compact and legible, then let hero moments breathe.
04
Always a next step.
Every screen tells a member what to do next. One primary action, never two.

09 — Components / Icons

Line icons. 2px stroke. 24px grid.

Consistent, minimal, translate-friendly. We draw icons, never emoji. Paired with a short text label whenever space allows.

Rules

  • — 24×24 viewBox, 2px rounded stroke
  • — Outline style only; no fills except for status dots
  • — Always center-aligned with adjacent text
  • — Match color to context (text-muted → inherits from label)
